The Agent Ecosystem

11 Coordinated Agents

Rather than relying on a single monolith prompt, SwasthyaOps orchestrates 11 distinct agents coordinated by a central Planner Agent.

Deterministic Guardrails

Agents operate only via strictly defined, typed tools. They can read inventory levels and calculate travel times, but cannot write databases or execute actions directly.

Forecast Agent: Handles stock-out math
Disease Agent: Predicts outbreaks
Doctor Agent: Tracks absenteeism
Briefing Agent: Creates the 07:00 IST report

Security, Compliance & Scale

DPDP Act 2023 Aligned
🔒 DPDP by Design

Zero Patient Personal Identifiable Information (PII) is processed. The system runs entirely on operational metadata (attendance, stock counts, bed counts).

📜 Auditable Log

Every single agent run, prompt template version, tool call, token length, and response is recorded in Firestore for full administrative auditability.

⚡ Low Cost & Scale

Serverless hosting costs are kept under ₹35,000 (~$400 USD) per district/month. Highly scalable to dozens of districts with zero architectural modifications.
